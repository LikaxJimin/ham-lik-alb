    • An idea for a t-shirt - 400 kg of waste


    I designd and produced a T-shirt about GreenPeace, showing its character and qualities. I was not allowed to use the company name or typo-graphic language. • One side of my t-shirt should contain only text. No images. • Another side of my t-shirt should contain only images (up to 10). No text. Images could be made by me or found. I was not allowed to use the company logo & graphic language or pictures-drawings of any products they make. • Both sides of my design had to be black and white.
    Soooooo, on their website I found an information that In one year a person makes 400kg of waste and it became my main idea.
